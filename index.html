About me


Thank you for visiting my page! My name is Kevin Santos. A little background about myself; I graduated in 2013 from UCLA with a B.S. in Civil Engineering, with focuses in structural and geotechnical engineering. Currently, I am working in Huntington Beach at the engineering consulting firm, Melad and Associates. My work is primarily focused on analyses of residential structures in accordance with state, federal and local ordinances as well as safe structural and civil engineering principles and practices. My company provides consulting services for several cities in Southern California including: Costa Mesa, Dana Point, Manhattan Beach, Monrovia, Southgate and Westminster.
